## 📖 About the project

This software helps users manage digital assets on the Solana network. It monitors price changes and executes trades based on user preferences. Traders use this tool to interact with decentralized exchanges like Jupiter, Orca, and Raydium. The bot identifies new tokens and manages buy or sell orders without manual input. It works as an automated assistant for your wallet.

## 🛠️ Configuring the bot

After the installation, you must configure your settings to allow the bot to interact with the blockchain.

*   Open the application from your desktop shortcut.
*   Enter your wallet public address into the settings menu.
*   Input your private key or select a keystore file for transaction signing. Keep this information private and secure. Do not share this key with anyone.
*   Set your slippage tolerance. This determines how much price variance you accept during a trade.
*   Define the maximum amount of Solana you wish to spend per trade.
*   Select the tokens you want to monitor.
*   Click Save to apply your preferences.

## 📈 Running the application

The bot handles several transaction types. You can view these in the trade interface.

*   Buy Orders: The bot detects a target price and executes a purchase.
*   Sell Orders: The bot monitors for profit targets and sells your position.
*   Monitoring: The interface keeps track of token price history.

If the application displays an error, check your internet connection first. Ensure your wallet has enough funds for transaction fees. Fees are paid in the native network coin. Low balances can cause transactions to fail.

## 📋 Security practices

Protecting your data remains a priority. Use these practices to stay safe.

*   Only download files from the provided link.
*   Do not share your private key or seed phrase with support staff or other users.
*   Use a separate wallet for bot trading rather than your main storage wallet.
*   Keep your operating system updated.
*   Check your wallet history frequently to verify bot activity.

The software functions locally on your machine. It does not send your private keys to remote servers. The bot signs transactions locally before broadcasting them to the network. This process ensures your keys never leave your controlled environment.
